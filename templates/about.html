About ChurnGuard

Learn about our mission, our team, and the story behind our AI-powered churn prediction technology

Our Story

ChurnGuard was founded in 2022 by a team of data scientists and customer retention experts who recognized a critical gap in the market. While businesses were investing heavily in customer acquisition, they lacked sophisticated tools to predict and prevent customer churn.

January 2022
The Idea Is Born

Our founders identified the need for an AI-driven solution to the costly problem of customer churn.

June 2022
First Prototype

We developed our first machine learning model capable of predicting churn with 98% accuracy.

March 2023
Beta Launch

ChurnGuard Beta was launched with 10 pilot customers across SaaS and subscription industries.

January 2024
Public Launch

After refining our algorithms, we officially launched ChurnGuard to the public.

Today
Growing Impact

ChurnGuard now serves over 500 businesses worldwide, saving our clients millions in potential lost revenue.

Our Mission

At ChurnGuard, our mission is to empower businesses with predictive intelligence that transforms customer retention. We believe that by identifying at-risk customers early, companies can take proactive steps to enhance customer experience, build stronger relationships, and maximize lifetime value.

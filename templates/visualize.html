Understanding the Visualizations

These visualizations provide critical insights into our model's performance and the key factors driving customer churn.

  • Confusion Matrix: Shows false positives/negatives and helps evaluate model accuracy
  • ROC Curve: Measures how well the model distinguishes between classes
  • Feature Importance: Identifies the most influential factors in churn prediction

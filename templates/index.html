Understanding Customer Churn

Customer churn refers to the loss of clients or customers. For subscription-based businesses, it's calculated as the percentage of service subscribers who discontinue their subscriptions within a given time period.

Why Predicting Churn Matters

Acquiring new customers can cost five times more than retaining existing ones. By identifying customers at risk of churning, companies can take proactive measures to improve retention and maximize customer lifetime value.
